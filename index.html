MEN OF CULTURE (M.O.C)

A podcast channel that ignited a whole new community

Movie review & discussion community

Movie enthusiasts and regular viewers have always loved talking about films. But, in the past, reviews were mostly done by expert critics who wrote fancy articles and gave ratings. Then, YouTube came along as a new platform. Everyday moviegoers got a chance to shine. For the first time, someone was speaking our language, not just writing fancy stuff. This made talking about movies feel more like chatting with a friend. And today we will be discussing a few of them famously known for their podcast Men of Culture.

Members OF M.O.C

1) Badal Yadav : Bnftv

Badal launched his YouTube channel with his friends, sharing videos of challenges and fun games. This spirit also shaped his channel's name, BNFTV, representing Badal and Friends TV. However, his breakthrough came when he delved into movie discussions. His first review of "Andhadhun" gave his channel the much-needed boost. Presently, he boasts 1.12 million subscribers on his main channel, BNFTV. He's also spearheaded several other channels, each with thousands and even lakhs of subscribers. Badal is the creative mind behind "Men of Culture," where he brought two other prominent YouTubers from the same community. Among his fans, he's lovingly referred to as the "business man."


2) Mohit : Comicverse

Mohit, a professional doctor, captivates his audience through his vast knowledge and intriguing personas like "Sanskari Mohit." His in-depth commentary on movies, particularly those based on comics and sci-fi, resonates deeply with viewers. Despite his medical background, his insights are beloved by many. Notably, Mohit recently tied the knot, and it was during this occasion that all three members of Men of Culture (MoC), who had previously collaborated on several podcasts, met in person for the first time. Fans affectionately refer to him as "Doctorshaab." He also spearheaded 2 other channels that are running successfully with his main channel leading with 1.22 million subscribers.

3) Priyanshu Jaiswal : Pj Explained

The youngest member of the MoC trio, Priyanshu, has surged ahead in terms of numbers. His innovative approach to YouTube shorts propelled him to become one of the foremost movie reviewers on the platform. Priyanshu's prowess is further evident through his various channels, with his primary channel boasting an impressive 2.02 million subscribers. His unique content delivery, insightful ideas, and choice of words resonate deeply with his audience. A popular fan quote has even become a catchphrase: "Pj never disappoints."
